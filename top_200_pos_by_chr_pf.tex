A chi-square test on the observed distribution of the 200 most strongly positively {\textit {COMT}}\den correlated genes in Prefrontal Cortex using the proportion of genes on each chromosome across the entire sample to derive the expected frequencies showed that this distribution did not differ significantly from the expected distribution, $\chi^2 = 28.874$, $df = 22$, $p = 0.16$.
